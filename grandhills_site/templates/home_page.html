GrandHills Welcomes You!

A company that owns a group of factories works in the ﬁeld of food and beverage manufacturing and Distributor for major pharmaceutical and nutritional supplement companies. Grand Hills was founded by a group of Egyptian investors in the state of Rwanda, in cooperation with local companies that were integrated into the group’s entity, thereby creating a large economic entity and a Rwandan national support that contributes to industrial growth within the country and is looking forward to neighboring countries.

Grand Hills Vision

Grand Hills Industries Group adopts an advanced approach in the foundations of work, quality, production efficiency and providing good job opportunities and national support as Grand Hills Industries works to improve the living conditions of its employees and for each region by allocating part of its production to charitable work.
